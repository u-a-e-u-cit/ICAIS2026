AI in Sports International Conference 2026

United Arab Emirates University

12th - 13th October 2026

About Conference

ICAIS 2026 is an international conference dedicated to the role of Artificial Intelligence in transforming sports performance, analytics, coaching, engagement, health, and management.

The conference features invited speakers from academia, industry, and the sports sector, along with student poster sessions and student competitions. The event aims to strengthen collaboration between academia and sports stakeholders including sports clubs, federations, governing bodies, and industry partners.

Program

Day 1

Opening & Invited Sessions

Opening ceremony, keynote talks, invited sessions, and student engagement activities.

Day 2

Posters & Competitions

Invited talks, student poster session, competitions, panel discussion, and closing remarks.

Topics

AI in Sports Themes

Sports analytics, computer vision, wearables, coaching, injury prevention, smart stadiums, and innovation.
